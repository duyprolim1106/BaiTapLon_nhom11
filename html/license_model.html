What Is Royalty-Free Music?

Royalty-free isn’t actually “free” in a monetary sense. What it means is that you don’t have to pay royalties to a music creator when using their music. However, the company you buy your license from can still pay royalties (from e.g. streaming) to their music creators, like Epidemic Sound does.

    Frequently Asked Questions
    What are royalites ?

    Royalties are the sums being paid to songwriters, producers, performing artists and musicians when their music is being played.

    What do the different music rights mean?

    Music rights are generally split into several categories:

    Synchronisation rights: The right to add the track to a video or moving image.

    Mechanical rights: The right to make copies of the audiovisual production including the track.

    Public performance rights: The right to make the audiovisual production, including
    the track, available to the public.

    What is a claim?

    A claim basically means that ads will be placed on your video and that Epidemic
    Sound will monetize the revenue generated from those ads.
